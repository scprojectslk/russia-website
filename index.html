Russia

Russia, also officially known as the Russian Federation, is a transcontinental country spanning Eastern Europe and Northern Asia. It is the largest country in the world by area, covering over 17 million square kilometers, and the ninth-most populous, with over 146 million inhabitants. Russia extends across eleven time zones and shares land borders with fourteen countries.

Official Language Russian

Cultural Review

Russian ballet is world-renowned for its grace, precision, and dramatic storytelling. Icons like Tchaikovsky's ballets continue to captivate audiences globally.

Saint Basil's Cathedral in Moscow is an iconic example of Russian Orthodox architecture, with its vibrant colors and unique onion domes.

The Russian tea ceremony, often involving a samovar, is a significant social tradition, emphasizing hospitality and conversation.
